
#### What is linear transformation?

It is a function for transforming vectors on a matrix. It has two key properties:
- All lines must remain straight lines without curving.
- The origin must remain fixed in place.
- Grid lines must be parallel and evenly spaced.

### Summary

Linear transformations are ways to move vectors in space, such that the lines remain parallel and the origin remains fixed.
