
#### What is a transformation? 

Transformation is a change in space, this can include a 'shear' or a 'rotation' for example.
Transformations can be done in both two and three dimensions, and would require either two or three inputs, based on the dimension.

Transformations should be done when the grid lines are still parallel and evenly spaced.


#### What is a basis vector?

There are three basis vectors, corresponding to different axis.

**î** = X
**ĵ** = Y
**k̂** = Z
