
#### What is composition?

Composition involves completing one operation or transformation right after another, 
for example, performing a rotation, and then a shear, would be a composition.

#### Matrix multiplication:

Matrix multiplication relates to the act of multiplying vectors or matrices by one another, this is usually associative, meaning any part can be done in any order, and the same outcome would be achieved.
